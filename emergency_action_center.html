Emergency Response Guide

Follow these steps for the fastest resolution

1

Immediate Reporting

Report your luggage issue immediately at the airport baggage service office or through our emergency hotline. Time is critical for the best outcome.

2

Documentation & Evidence

Gather all relevant documents: boarding passes, baggage receipts, and photos of damage. Our system will guide you through the documentation process.

3

Active Monitoring

We'll actively monitor your case and provide regular updates. You'll receive notifications at every step of the resolution process.

4

Resolution & Compensation

Once resolved, we'll help you claim any compensation you're entitled to and ensure you're fully satisfied with the outcome.
